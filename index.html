About Me

I'm Jozan, a data analyst with an interest in supply chain. I graduated from SMK Tujuh Lima 1 Purwokerto majoring in Multimedia and have experience in data analysis for 1 year and warehouse for 4 years.
My skills include statistical analysis, data processing, and data visualization using Excel, SQL, and Looker Studio.
I have a basic understanding of supply chain management and how data can be used to optimize processes.
In the last project, I succeeded in improving process efficiency by analyzing data and providing data-based recommendations. I am committed to continuing to develop my analytical skills and contribute to supply chain optimization.
In my spare time, I like to follow the latest developments in the field of data and supply chain.

If you have any questions or would like to discuss further, don't hesitate to contact me!
