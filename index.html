About Me

Welcome! I'm Jozan, a passionate data analyst with a keen interest in supply chain management. I graduated from SMK Tujuh Lima 1 Purwokerto, majoring in Multimedia, and I bring 1 year of experience in data analysis along with 4 years in warehouse operations.

My skills include statistical analysis, data processing, and data visualization using Excel, SQL, and Looker Studio. I am particularly interested in how data-driven insights can enhance decision-making in supply chain processes. With a foundational understanding of supply chain management, I see great potential in leveraging data to optimize logistics, inventory management, and demand forecasting.

In my most recent project, I successfully enhanced process efficiency by analyzing data and providing data-driven recommendations. I am committed to continuously developing my analytical skills and aspire to contribute to innovative solutions in supply chain management.

In my spare time, I enjoy keeping up with the latest developments in the fields of data and supply chain.

If you have any questions or would like to discuss further, please don't hesitate to reach out!
